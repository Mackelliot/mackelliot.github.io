About

Perk Up, Grant! started the way many great ideas do—somewhere after a fourth cup of coffee but before a fourth cup of wine. After rewatching all seasons of Bluey for the 9th time without any good next things to watch, a weary dad hit his limit. So, naturally, he did the only reasonable thing left: he opened a coffee shop in his house.

What began as a small setup—just him, a stubborn espresso machine, and a dream—quickly became a local favorite. But there was a cost. Mainly, he could no longer say “Oh sorry, I don’t work here” when his in-laws asked him to unclog the sink.

Today, Perk Up, Grant! isn’t just a place for coffee—it’s a place for connection. It’s about love, family, and the people who show up for each other. One cup at a time.
