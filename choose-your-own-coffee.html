🎉 Welcome to Perk Up, Grant!

You apply for an opening as a barista at Perk Up, Grant!, a coffee company that takes caffeine — and drama — very seriously.

Just as you hit "submit," your computer screen flickers. Smoke pours from the USB port. A squirrel in a tiny apron bursts through your window yelling, “The Roast Awakens!”

What do you do?

🐿️ Into the Brew-niverse

The squirrel leads you to a glowing espresso machine in an alley. You touch it. Time warps. Your name is now "Café Jones."

Do you:

💋 Espresso in Venice

You’re whisked to Venice, where a sexy Italian man named Lorenzo teaches you to make the perfect affogato. You fall in love and start a gondola café.

Application status: Tragically romantic. Not hired.

🦖 Jurassic Perk

The steam wand was actually a time portal. You wake up in the Cretaceous era and are immediately eaten by a dinosaur named Kevin.

Application status: Digested. Not hired.

📩 Digital Disaster

You open an email from "HR Bot 4000." It says: “To complete your application, you must submit a song.”

Do you:

🎤 Campus Cover Kings

You join a Backstreet Boys cover band called “Snackstreet Boys.” You tour college campuses during lunch hours. Your “I Want It Barista Way” goes viral.

Application status: Millennium-style fame. Not hired.

🚀 Mars or Bust

Your chant is misunderstood by NASA as a launch code. You are launched into space. You colonize Mars, start the first alien espresso bar. Elon Musk owes you rent.

Application status: Intergalactic. Not hired.

👻 Haunted Grounds

You inherit a haunted espresso machine. Each cappuccino conjures a ghost who critiques your foam art. You're now a medium, not a barista.

Application status: Spiritually overqualified. Not hired.

🧬 Mutant Barista

A freak cold brew accident mutates you. You grow six arms and become a latte machine. Customers scream. Yelp reviews are... mixed.

Application status: Caffeinated chimera. Not hired.

🕵️‍♂️ Operation: Steep Secret

The CIA recruits you after they hear your chant. Your mission: infiltrate Belgian cafés to uncover the black tea cartel. You’re a spy now.

Application status: Classified. Not hired.
